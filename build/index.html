Human Development of US Communities

The Human Development Index (HDI) is the international gold standard of a person’s ability to lead a long, healthy life with access to knowledge and a decent standard of living. It is a composite of life expectancy, education, and real income.

We measured it in every US local community to compare them to international levels of development.
